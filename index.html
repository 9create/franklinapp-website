Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents want to know

What will my child actually create?

Your child will build 2-3 real Android applications and publish them on Google Play Store with their name as the developer. These are actual, working apps‚Äînot practice projects or simulations. Examples include utility apps, games, educational tools, or creative applications based on your child's interests.

Why only 4 students per batch?

Small batch sizes ensure every child gets personal attention, hands-on guidance, and guaranteed learning outcomes. With only 4 students, the instructor can work closely with each child, address individual challenges, and ensure no one falls behind. This isn't a lecture‚Äîit's a mentorship.

Does my child need prior coding experience?

Not at all! This program is designed for complete beginners. We start from the basics and build up gradually. If your child can use a computer and is curious about technology, they're ready to start. Ages 8-12 are perfect for beginning this journey.

What if my child misses a class?

We provide makeup classes and recorded sessions so your child doesn't miss any content. Learning is flexible, and we ensure every student completes all modules regardless of occasional absences.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! Your child receives a certificate of completion. But more importantly, they get their app published on Google Play Store‚Äîa real credential that showcases their skills. Their published app is proof of what they've learned, far more valuable than any certificate.

What equipment does my child need?

Just a laptop or computer with internet connection. We'll guide you through any necessary software installation (all free tools). No special hardware or expensive equipment required.
